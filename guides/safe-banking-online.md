# Safe Banking Online

## Before You Log In
- Make sure you're on the official bank website or app.
- Don't use public Wi‑Fi for banking.
- Check for the padlock icon (https://).

## While You're Logged In
- Never share your password or SMS codes with anyone.
- Log out when you're done, especially on shared devices.

## After Banking
- Review your transactions regularly.
- Set up alerts for transactions (many banks offer free SMS alerts).

## If You Get a Suspicious Call or Message
- Hang up. Don't click.
- Call your bank using the number on your card.

**Remember:** Your bank will never ask for your full password or PIN by phone, email, or text.
