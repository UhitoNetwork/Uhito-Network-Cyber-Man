# Printable Safety Checklist

Print this page and put it on your fridge.

---

## ✅ Every Day
- [ ] Did I get any strange messages? (don't click)
- [ ] Did I log out of accounts on shared computers?

## ✅ Every Week
- [ ] Check bank and credit card statements for unknown charges
- [ ] Run software updates

## ✅ Every Month
- [ ] Review who has access to your social media accounts
- [ ] Change important passwords if you think they might be compromised

## ✅ Once a Year
- [ ] Check your credit report
- [ ] Review all online accounts and close unused ones

---

**If something feels wrong, stop and ask someone you trust.**
