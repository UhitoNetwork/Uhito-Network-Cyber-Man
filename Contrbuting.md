# Contributing to Uhito Network Awareness

We welcome:
- Suggestions for new warnings or stories
- Translations of existing content
- Feedback from community workshops

We do NOT accept:
- Pull requests that copy our content elsewhere
- Requests to remove attribution
- Commercial collaboration offers

## How to Contribute
1. Open an Issue with your idea
2. Tell us about your experience using these materials
3. If you translate content, we'll add it to the repo with your name
