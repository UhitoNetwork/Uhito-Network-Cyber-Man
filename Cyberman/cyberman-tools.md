# CYBER-MAN TOOLS

## What They Use & Know

### Knowledge Tools:
1. **Certified Learning** – Official certificates (Security+, CEH, etc.)
2. **Threat Patterns** – How to recognize common scams
3. **Simple Explanations** – Ways to make complex things understandable
4. **Legal Frameworks** – What's allowed with certification

### Communication Tools:
- **Stories** – Real cases turned into lessons
- **Analogies** – "Digital locks" instead of "encryption"
- **Simple Checklists** – Basic safety steps anyone can follow
- **Visual Guides** – Before/after examples

### Resource Tools:
- **Official Contacts** – Police cyber units, government agencies
- **Certified Professionals** – For referrals when needed
- **Legal Training Platforms** – Where to learn properly
- **Community Networks** – Other protectors to learn from

### No Hacking Tools:
- No attack software
- No unauthorized access methods
- No illegal testing tools
- Only defensive, legal knowledge
